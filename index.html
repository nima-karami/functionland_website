We believe in a fairer future, in the redistribution of power, and in privacy.

We are on a the mission to deliver on sustainable data sovereignty for the mainstream by expanding Web3 beyond DeFi into everyday applications. We put an end to the “Rental Web” by providing people with the infrastructure to own it and democratize access to cutting edge technologies like AI and Blockchain. We aim to create an open ecosystem for software/hardware developers through the secret sauce of "Monetized Open Source”. We design from the first principles for “privacy” and “ethics” to be the foundation, not an afterthought.

The amount people pay in Cloud subscriptions is exploding. Further, 50% express that they are not satisfied with the balance of convenience over the costs imposed, with alternatives being exposed to ads or being subject to data harvesting without consent.

Every Borg user participates in hosting Web3 through an owned miner. In this new paradigm, traditional Cloud services are replaced by resource sharing through Blockchain. All parties involved are compensated. We will be the first to introduce paid open source development in the industry.
